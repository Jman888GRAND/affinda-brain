# Strategic Planning

**Status:** 📄 CV only

## Definition
The ability to define long-term goals, identify the steps and resources required to reach them, and adapt plans as circumstances evolve.

## Evidence
- Designed and executed a multi-year development plan for the Huntingtower Boys' Volleyball Program, progressing the team from 15th to 7th to 3rd at the National Schools Cup across consecutive years
- Co-founded Liu activewear with a deliberate product and brand strategy targeting the natural fiber activewear market, building with AI tools from the outset
- Built AffindaBrain as a planned career intelligence infrastructure project, with a defined pipeline, schema, and long-term knowledge compounding logic
