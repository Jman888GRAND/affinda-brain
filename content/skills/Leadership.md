# Leadership

**Status:** ✅ Matched

## Definition
The ability to direct, motivate, and develop others toward a shared goal, taking ownership of outcomes and decisions in a team or organisational context.

## Evidence
- Head of Boys' Volleyball Program at Huntingtower School, managing 30+ athletes, designing training programs, and leading the team to improve from 15th to 3rd at the National Schools Cup
- Delivered a 35% increase in program participation, requiring ongoing recruitment, retention, and culture-building beyond pure coaching
- Acted as interim manager at Woolworths Deli when leadership gaps arose, coordinating frontline staff and maintaining operational continuity
