# Criminology

## Definition

The academic study of crime, criminal behaviour, law enforcement, and justice systems. Covers theories of deviance, penology, victimology, and the social structures that shape criminal outcomes.

## Evidence

- Lukas Tsirigotis is completing a Bachelor of Criminology & Arts at Monash University (2023–present), developing formal analytical frameworks around law, regulation, and social behaviour
