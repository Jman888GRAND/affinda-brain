# Game Theory

**Status:** 📄 CV only

## Definition
The application of mathematical and strategic frameworks that model decision-making among rational agents with interdependent outcomes, used to predict behaviour and optimise strategy.

## Evidence
- Explicitly applied game theory to lineup and rotation decisions as Head of Boys' Volleyball at Huntingtower School, treating matches as strategic interactions and optimising choices accordingly
- The program's results improved from 15th to 3rd at the National Schools Cup, a measurable outcome tied to this applied strategic approach
- PPE studies at Monash include formal exposure to game theoretic concepts within economic analysis coursework
