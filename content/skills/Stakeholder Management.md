# Stakeholder Management

**Status:** 📄 CV only

## Definition
The ability to identify, engage, and align diverse stakeholders with different interests and priorities to advance a project or organisational goal.

## Evidence
- Virtual Producer at Lysander Consulting, managing communications and production workflows for enterprise clients including ANZ, requiring careful navigation of client expectations in high-stakes settings
- Head of Boys' Volleyball Program at Huntingtower School, coordinating across athletes, school administration, and parents to sustain a 30+ person program and drive a 35% participation increase
- Co-founding Liu required engaging suppliers, collaborators, and potential customers across multiple functions simultaneously
