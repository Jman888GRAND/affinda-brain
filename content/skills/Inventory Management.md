# Inventory Management

## Definition

The process of ordering, storing, tracking, and controlling stock to ensure products are available when needed without excess or wastage. Includes stocktake, goods receiving, and warehouse organisation.

- Lukas Tsirigotis performed stocktake and inventory control across multiple retail roles, including a sporting goods store and a toys/educational retailer, with responsibility for FoH, POS, and BoH stock processes
