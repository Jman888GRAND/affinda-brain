# General Pants Co

**Type:** Australian fashion retail chain
**Headquarters:** Sydney, Australia
**Category:** Youth and streetwear apparel

## What They Do

General Pants Co is an Australian fashion retailer specialising in denim, streetwear, and youth culture brands. They stock a curated mix of international and local labels — Levi's, Nudie Jeans, Dr. Martens, Carhartt, and others — operating stores across major shopping centres and CBDs. Known for strong brand identity and a culture-forward retail environment.
