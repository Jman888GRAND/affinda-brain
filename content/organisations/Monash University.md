# Monash University

**Type:** Public research university
**Location:** Clayton, Melbourne, Victoria (main campus)
**Ranking:** Top 50 globally (QS World University Rankings)
**Category:** Group of Eight (Go8) research-intensive university

## What They Do

Monash University is one of Australia's leading research universities and a member of the prestigious Group of Eight. It offers programs across business, law, medicine, engineering, arts, and social sciences, with a strong international research reputation and campuses in Malaysia and beyond.
