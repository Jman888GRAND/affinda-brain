# Affinda Group

**Type:** Australian-owned private technology company
**Headquarters:** Melbourne, Australia
**Size:** ~100 people
**Customers:** 2,000+ across 80 countries

## What They Do

Affinda Group is an AI document intelligence company. Their core technology extracts structured data from unstructured documents — resumes, invoices, receipts, contracts — using machine learning models trained on millions of real-world documents. Results are returned as structured JSON via API, making document processing programmable at scale.

Their product portfolio spans multiple verticals:

- **Affinda** — document AI for HR and recruitment (resume parsing, job description extraction, candidate matching)
- **Draftable** — document comparison software for legal and professional services
- **Pathfindr** — AI-powered tools for career intelligence and workforce planning
- **Vesparum Capital** — corporate advisory and capital markets

## Why It Matters to This Brain

AffindaBrain was built using the Affinda resume parsing API. Every candidate and job node in this graph was extracted by Affinda's models — structured JSON responses are stored in `raw/` and compiled into the wiki by Claude. Affinda provides the extraction layer; the graph and analysis layer is what AffindaBrain adds on top.
