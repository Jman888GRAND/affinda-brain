# Huntingtower School

**Type:** Independent co-educational school
**Location:** Mount Waverley, Melbourne, Victoria
**Category:** Primary and secondary education (Prep – Year 12)

## What They Do

Huntingtower is an independent co-educational school in Melbourne's eastern suburbs offering education through to VCE. Known for strong academic outcomes, co-curricular programs, and a broad sporting culture — the school competes at state and national level across multiple sports.

## Connection to Candidates

Both James and Lukas Tsirigotis attended Huntingtower for VCE. James returned as Head of Boys' Volleyball, taking the team from 15th to 3rd at the National Schools Cup.
