# Woolworths

**Type:** Public company (ASX: WOW)
**Headquarters:** Bella Vista, New South Wales
**Category:** Supermarket and grocery retail
**Scale:** ~1,000 stores across Australia, ~200,000 employees

## What They Do

Woolworths Group is Australia's largest supermarket chain, operating Woolworths Supermarkets, Big W, and other retail formats. The core supermarket business handles fresh food, grocery, and household product retail at scale across major cities and regional centres nationwide.
